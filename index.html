List of Movies

  • Avengers: Endgame

    Release Year: 2019

    Summary: After the devastating events of Avengers: Infinity War, the universe is in ruins. The remaining Avengers must assemble once again to undo Thanos' actions and restore order to the universe.

  • Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge

    Release Year: 1995

    Summary: Raj and Simran, two young individuals from different cultural backgrounds, meet during a trip across Europe. As they fall in love, they must overcome various obstacles to be together.

    Release Year: 2015

    Summary: Set in the fictional kingdom of Mahishmati, the film follows the journey of Shivudu, a young man who discovers his true identity and embarks on a quest to rescue the captive princess and reclaim his kingdom.

  • The Dark Knight

    Release Year: 2008

    Summary: Batman, along with the help of Commissioner Gordon and the new District Attorney, Harvey Dent, sets out to dismantle the remaining criminal organizations in Gotham City. However, their plans are disrupted by the chaotic and unpredictable Joker.

  • Vikram Vedha

    Release Year: 2017

    Summary: A gritty crime thriller set in the backdrop of Chennai city, the film revolves around the cat-and-mouse game between a notorious gangster named Vedha and a determined police officer named Vikram.

  • La La Land

    Release Year: 2016

    Summary: Sebastian, a jazz pianist, and Mia, an aspiring actress, meet and fall in love in Los Angeles. As they chase their dreams, they face various obstacles and must make difficult choices that threaten their relationship.
